Welcome to Kerala

Kerala, often referred to as "God's Own Country," is a paradise for travelers with its diverse and breathtaking tourist spots. The serene backwaters of Alleppey and Kumarakom, with their houseboat cruises, offer a tranquil escape. Munnar, Wayanad, and Thekkady are known for their lush green hills, tea plantations, and wildlife sanctuaries. The pristine beaches of Kovalam, Varkala, and Marari attract visitors looking for relaxation and water activities.

Alleppey Backwaters

Experience the serene beauty of Kerala’s backwaters as you glide through calm waters on a traditional houseboat in Alleppey.

The lush greenery and peaceful atmosphere make it a must-visit destination.

Munnar Hills

Munnar is a picturesque hill station known for its rolling tea plantations, cool climate, and breathtaking views of the Western Ghats.

It’s an ideal spot for nature lovers and honeymooners.

Kovalam Beach

Kovalam Beach is a popular destination for sunbathing, swimming, and enjoying Ayurvedic massages along its pristine shores.

The lighthouse adds charm to its stunning coastline.

Thekkady Wildlife Sanctuary

Thekkady is home to the Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary, where you can enjoy boat safaris and spot elephants, tigers, and diverse bird species.

It’s a paradise for wildlife enthusiasts.
